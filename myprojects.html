Digital Library

Debashish Jena
04 March

The purpose of the digital library management system is to ease digital library management and to create a convenient and easy-to-use digital library for learners, trying to get digital educational content. The system is based on a relational database with its digital library management and access functions. We will have a database server storing thousands of soft copies of books, video tutorials. Above all, we hope to provide a comfortable and easy to use digital library.

In Modern era of digitalization, a wide range of online services are providing to the users by the most of the libraries. Now it is time for a library to be virtual and develop its on-line availability in order to further facilitate and enrich the education process. And, the internet and web technologies are not the new things to any academician. Digital libraries are the new vision of libraries of the future.

Digital Libraries provide a new way of serving the new generation users of the libraries. In this paper, authors provide an overview of a digital Library System and narrate the purpose, features, functions design and development of a Digital Library and Digital Library Environment. Impact of ‘Digital Library’ on ‘Lifelong Learning’ has also been emphasized.

They also enumerate principles of development of Digital Library Collection and advantages of Digital Libraries.
